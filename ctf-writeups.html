Base64 Encoding Trap
Crypto / Misc Easy
▼

Base64 is NOT encryption — it's just encoding. Encrypted data looks like random Base64. Encoded data can be decoded by anyone.

Steps
  1. 1. String: RmxhZ3tZMHVfMG5seV9kZWNvZGV9
  2. 2. Looks like gibberish \u2014 must be encrypted?
  3. 3. Try: echo 'RmxhZ3tZMHVfMG5seV9kZWNvZGV9' | base64 -d
  4. 4. Output: Flag{Y0u_0nly_decode}
  5. 5. It was just Base64!
